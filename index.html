Guia

is a traveler-to-guide matching application for tourists and local guides

Features

The things for you guys to look forward to. Those who very much love traveling and like to experience the culture of the Philippines first hand.

Connect

Connect with the right local guides for a more memorable and immersive experience.

Safety

All local guides go through a background check to ensure the safety of travelers.

Discover

Go off the beaten path for awesome and unique experiences.

Guia is trying to promote the culture of the Philippines at the same time help local guides earn some living for doing what they love.
